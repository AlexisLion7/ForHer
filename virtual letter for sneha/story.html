Our Long-Distance Story

How It Started

A birthday message I was too nervous to send, yet I did. A quiet confession that I had a crush on you. You smiled. You stayed. And over time, something changed. I started growing closer to the girl I once admired from a distance. And when you finally told me you felt it too, that you liked me back, it was the sweetest moment.

Held By Time Zones

You say good morning while I whisper goodnight. And yet our hearts meet in the middle. With sleepy smiles, with surprise calls, in the way your voice can calm storms in me you do not even know are there.

Dreaming Forward

I imagine us in the same room. Your legs tucked under mine. My head resting on your chest. Feeling your smile on my skin, hearing your voice fill the space we finally share. Until that day comes, I will hold on to the wait. And when I finally wake up to you, not my phone, not a message, just you. It will feel like I have found my way home.
